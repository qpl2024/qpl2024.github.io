Excursion

We are organizing an excursion to the Delta de Tigre on Wednesday, the 17th of July.

Planning

The excursion starts at 3:00 pm, after lunch, with a bus trip departing from the conference venue.

We arrive at Tigre around 3:30 pm. You will have one-hour to visit this small but picturesque city.

At 4:30 pm we depart from Tigre Fluvial Station in a boat with audio guide. Navigation is about 2 hours through several rivers and canals where we can appreciate the particular way of living in houses on the river. Then we enter the Río de la Plata, the widest river in the world, to dock at Puerto Madero around 6:30 pm.

We arrive at Puerto Madero, in Buenos Aires City, around 6:30 pm. Puerto Madero is one of the most modern and exclusive neighbourhoods in Buenos Aires, with a wide variety of bars and pubs. The excursion ends here, and you can stay in the area to have dinner or return to the hotel. We will provide you of a map with some pubs and bars in the area, so those who do not participate in the excursion, can join us for a drink.
