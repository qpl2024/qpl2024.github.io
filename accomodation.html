Accomodation

There are many hotels within a 2 km radius of the conference venue. You should consider the following neighbourhoods: Belgrano, Núñez, and the north of Palermo.

The Belgrano Norte train line has a station on the university campus where the conference will take place. However, be aware that Retiro station, near the beginning of the line, is not the best place to stay.
It is 4 km from the conference venue to metro line D, which will take you to the city centre and connect you with other metro lines. A midpoint between that station and the venue could also be a good place to stay.

Negotiated prices

We have negotiated a small discount at some hotels.

Monroe Suites Hotel (apart hotel)

Monroe Suites Hotel is an apart hotel with rooms equipped with kitchenette and microwave, located in the Belgrano neighbourhood (see map above).
You can book your room with a 5% discount compared to the best available rate published on their website by sending a WhatsApp message to +54 9 11 3677-7788 and presenting your QPL registration.

Pampa Plaza Hotel (apart hotel)

Hotel Pampa Plaza is located in the Belgrano neighbourhood (see map above).
You can book a single room with a kitchenette, balcony, and breakfast included for USD 120 per night, taxes included (about a 10% discount over their published prices), by sending an email to info@hotelpampaplaza.com.ar and mentioning the code "QPL2024".
