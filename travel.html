EZE: Buenos Aires International Airport

The Ministro Pistarini International Airport (commonly known as "Ezeiza Airport") is located 22 km south-southwest of Buenos Aires, in the city of Ezeiza. It is the country's largest international airport and a hub for the international routes of Aerol√≠neas Argentinas. The airport has three terminals: one for international arrivals, one for international departures, and one for domestic flights. The safest and easiest way to get to and from the airport is by airport transfers and taxis.

AEP: The domestic flights airport

The Jorge Newbery Airport (commonly known as "Aeroparque") is located in the city, merely 3 km from the conference venue. This airport operates most domestic and regional flights and can be accessed by public transportation such as urban buses and taxis.
