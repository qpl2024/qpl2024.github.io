Visa information

Visa requirements

QPL 2024 will be held in Argentina. A visa may be required to enter Argentina, depending on the country issuing your passport. Please check this site from the National Direction of Migrations of Argentina to determine whether you need a visa or not.

If you require a visa and have already registered for the conference, we can provide you with visa support letters. Only authors of submitted papers, talks, posters, or tools, and members of the program committee can request a visa support letter prior to registering for the conference.

QPL 2024 is organized jointly by Universidad Nacional de Quilmes and Universidad de Buenos Aires. The support letters are issued by the former.
