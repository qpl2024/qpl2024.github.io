Social events

Conference dinner

The conference dinner will be at Galidon, a traditional Argentine restaurant. The dinner will be held on Thursday, the 18th of July, at 8:00 pm. The cost of the dinner is USD 50.
The menu consists of a typical Argentinian "parrillada" with dessert, with one bottle of Argentinian Malbec wine for every 2 persons, and complimentary non-alcoholic drinks.

There are also vegan, vegetarian, and gluten-free options (to be communicated in the registration form).

Excursion

We are organizing an excursion to the Delta del Tigre on Wednesday, the 17th of July. The cost of the excursion is USD 110.

The excursion starts at 2pm, after lunch, with a bus trip departing from the conference venue.

We arrive at Puerto de Frutos around 3pm, an excellent place to buy souvenirs, have some drinks, and enjoy the view of the river. But also, you can simply take a walk at Tigre streets, which is a quite pintoresque city to visit.

At 4:30pm we depart from Tigre Fluvial Station in a modern boat with audio guide. Navigation is about 2 hours through several rivers where we can appreciate the particular way of living in houses on the river. Then we enter the RÃ­o de la Plata, the widest river in the world, to dock at Puerto Madero around 6:30pm.

We arrive at Puerto Madero, in Buenos Aires City, around 6:30pm. Puerto Madero is one of the most modern and exclusive neighborhoods in Buenos Aires, with a wide variety of bars and pubs. The excursion ends here, and you can stay in the area to have dinner or return to the hotel.
